Village Gift Catalog

  1. Home
  2. Village Gift Catalog

Help village families change their lives through the Village Gift program!

100% of the funds you contribute will go directly into the Village Development program, helping families obtain clean water, farm animals, vegetable gardens, fishponds and beehives. Village families who become part of the Pass-On Program are empowered to become donors themselves: When their animals have grown, or their beehive or fishpond is thriving, the family passes on an identical gift to two other families, and those families later do the same. So your gift keeps on growing through the years, creating positive change by empowerment of village families.

Each gift you choose can be made in your name, or that of a friend or family member. You can choose as many gifts as you wish. For each gift we'll send you a thank-you card, and send one to the friend or family member in whose name you gave.
